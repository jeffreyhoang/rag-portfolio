# Youth Basketball Coach — Sacramento Asian Sports Foundation

**Organization:** Sacramento Asian Sports Foundation, Sacramento, CA
**Duration:** May 2020 – July 2022
**Type:** Volunteer coaching role

## What I Did

- Designed and led structured practice sessions for groups of 8 to 12 athletes, teaching basketball fundamentals through drills, demonstrations, and real-time feedback
- Kept sessions organized, energetic, and on schedule while adapting pace and style to each group's needs
- Emphasized teamwork, responsibility, sportsmanship, and discipline alongside athletic development
- Communicated regularly with parents and guardians to coordinate schedules and set expectations
- Motivated players with patience and encouragement, reinforcing the connection between hard work and improvement

## Skills Developed

Leadership, structured communication, mentorship, adaptive teaching, youth development
