# Skills Basketball Coach — Silicon Valley Soldiers

**Organization:** Silicon Valley Soldiers (Nike EYBL Oakland Soldiers affiliate), San Jose, CA
**Duration:** Current
**Type:** Coaching role, competitive youth basketball

## About the Organization

The Silicon Valley Soldiers operate under the Nike EYBL Oakland Soldiers umbrella — one of the South Bay's most respected youth basketball programs. Founded from BMG Basketball Academy (San Jose, 2015), merged with Nike EYBL Oakland Soldiers in 2021. 15+ teams, 150+ players across age groups.

## What I Do

- Deliver structured skills training sessions for groups and individual athletes at the 10U–14U age groups
- Focus on basketball fundamentals, player development, and competition preparation for high school and college pathways
- Adapt coaching style across age groups and skill levels, pushing each athlete toward their ceiling
- Operate within the organization's philosophy: hard work, character development, and life skills alongside basketball training

## Skills Developed

Leadership, structured communication, adaptive teaching, motivating individuals within a high-expectation team culture, working with youth athletes at a competitive level
