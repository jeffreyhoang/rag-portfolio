# Machine Learning Research Intern — STARS Program

**Organization:** Computational Intelligence Lab, Cal Poly Pomona
**Supervisor:** Dr. Hao Ji, Ph.D., Associate Professor
**Duration:** June 2024 – August 2024
**Type:** Undergraduate research internship

## Focus Areas

Multi-view computer vision, markerless human pose estimation, and end-to-end ML pipeline development.

## What I Did

- Conducted a structured literature review on multi-view geometry and markerless human pose estimation to inform system design and modeling choices
- Designed and built an automated end-to-end computer vision pipeline: intrinsic and extrinsic camera calibration, multi-view video preprocessing, 2D keypoint detection, and 3D pose reconstruction
- Implemented a robust audio-based multi-camera synchronization system using Librosa, FFmpeg, and NumPy to automate temporal alignment and eliminate frame offset errors
- Presented research methodology and experimental results at the Creative Activities and Research Symposium (CARS) at Cal Poly Pomona

## Technologies

Python, Librosa, FFmpeg, NumPy, multi-view geometry, camera calibration, 2D and 3D pose estimation, HPC infrastructure
