# Student Research Assistant — Computational Intelligence Lab

**Organization:** Department of Computer Science, Cal Poly Pomona
**Supervisor:** Dr. Hao Ji, Ph.D., Associate Professor
**Duration:** August 2024 – December 2025
**Type:** Research engineering role

## Focus Areas

Computer vision, human pose estimation, and deep learning infrastructure within an academic high-performance computing environment.

## What I Did

- Developed web-based automation tools and REST APIs to control multi-camera GoPro setups using Python, OpenGoPro API, USB, BLE, and Wi-Fi — significantly increasing data collection efficiency
- Trained deep neural networks for 3D keypoint detection on the Human3.6M dataset using TensorFlow on NVIDIA A100 GPUs across HPC clusters, applying batch normalization, ReLU activation, dropout, and linear layers
- Developed a physics-based pipeline fitting Metrabs 3D keypoint predictions to biomechanical LocoMuJoCo models using forward kinematics and optimization to estimate joint angles and body scaling parameters
- Results produced anatomically consistent and physically accurate 3D human pose alignments for downstream biomechanics analysis and simulation

## Technologies

Python, TensorFlow, OpenGoPro API, NVIDIA A100, HPC clusters, forward kinematics, MuJoCo, Human3.6M dataset
